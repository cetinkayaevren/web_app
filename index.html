MICHEAL JORDAN (1984-2013)

   Michael Jeffrey Jordan was born on 17 February 1963 in Brooklyn,New York. 
He is known that is the one of the best basketball player in the world.
His father,James Raymond Jordan (July 31, 1936 - July 23, 1993), who was
a big basketball fan played a large role for inspiring to Micheal Jordan
to become basketball player. Jordan's hobbies were football,basketball 
and baseball.

KOBE BRYANT (1996-2016)

 


   Kobe Bryant whose nickname is "Black Mamba" was an one of the most 
important player in American basketball history. He was born on 23 August
1973 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. His father,John Bryant, was a basketball
player. Due to his father he started to play basketball at 3 years old. Also,
he can speak Italian and Spanish because his father played basketball in Europe.
HE broke a lot of records in his basketball history.
